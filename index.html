A 6-Year Digital Journey
Every year builds on the last.

From JSS 1 to SSS 3, students graduate with portfolio-ready digital skills and an IIT certificate at every level.

Junior Secondary (JSS 1 – 3)

JSS 1
Microsoft Office + AI
Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Microsoft Copilot. Students master the world's most widely used productivity suite powered by AI from day one.
🎯 Outcome: A formatted Word report and PowerPoint presentation
JSS 2
Google Workspace + Gemini AI
Docs, Sheets, Drive, Meet, and Gemini AI. Students collaborate on live group projects using the tools powering millions of organisations globally.
🎯 Outcome: A live collaborative Google Workspace group project
JSS 3
Canva Design + Website Dev
Graphic design, brand kits, and Canva websites. Students discover the power of visual communication and create professional digital content with AI.
🎯 Outcome: A complete brand identity and published Canva website

Senior Secondary (SSS 1 – 3)

SSS 1
Product Design — UI/UX + AI
Design thinking, Figma, wireframing, and AI tools. Students learn to design digital products people love — one of Africa's most in-demand skills.
🎯 Outcome: A complete mobile app prototype designed in Figma
SSS 2
Website Development + AI
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and GitHub Copilot. Students build real, functional websites from scratch and publish them live to the internet.
🎯 Outcome: A live published personal or business website
SSS 3
Digital Marketing
SEO, social media strategy, paid ads, and analytics. Students learn to grow businesses and brands online — a skill companies are aggressively hiring for.
🎯 Outcome: A fully executed live digital marketing campaign
